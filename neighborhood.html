Districts of Ho Chi Minh City

The city is divided into several districts, each with its own charm. District 1 is the commercial center with luxury hotels and landmarks, while District 3 is known for its French colonial houses and local cafés.

District 5, also called Chợ Lớn, is home to the city’s vibrant Chinatown, offering an entirely different cultural experience.
